Memories of Chiyoda

Posted on October 14, 2025

Sometimes I wake up in the middle of the night reaching for my work phone, expecting a call from Sanada-san. Old habits die hard.

Living in Tokyo was an incredible experience for a young engineer. The discipline, the precision... it shapes you. But working for The Firm (as we used to call the enterprise in Chiyoda-ku) demanded a piece of your soul in exchange for that salary. I don't regret leaving. The "requests" were becoming... unreasonable. I am not a black hat. I build systems; I don't break people.

I kept this photo to remind me why I left. The lights are beautiful, but they hide too much. Malaga is different. The sun here cleanses everything. Or so I hope.
